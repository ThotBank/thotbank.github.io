ThotBank

This website and the related database are under development

ThotBank, a part of the Madùwwe project, is a dataset of Coptic words for which an Egyptian etymology has been suggested. The datased provides the Coptic form in unicode, and automatically generated trascription, translation based on the Comprehensive Coptic Lexicon project, the suggested Egyptian etymology with references, a translation of the Egyptian form based on the Thesaurus Linguae Aegyptiae project, and additional related Coptic and Egyptian words.

Dialectal variants of the Coptic forms will be added soon.

The project is currently in developement, and new entries and new references are added regularly.
